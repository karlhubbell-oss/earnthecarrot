Northpeak Systems

FY2025 Account Executive Compensation Plan

Effective January 1, 2025 – December 31, 2025

1. Overview

This plan governs on-target earnings for the Account Executive (AE) role at Northpeak Systems for fiscal year 2025. Variable compensation is earned as a percentage of booked revenue across the AE’s assigned revenue motions, subject to the attainment floor and terms below.

2. Base Salary and On-Target Earnings

  • Base salary: $140,000 per year
  • Target variable (commission at 100% of quota): $120,000
  • On-Target Earnings (OTE): $260,000
  • Pay mix: approximately 54% base / 46% variable

3. Annual Quota and Components

The AE carries a total annual quota of $2,500,000, allocated across two revenue motions. Each component is measured against its own quota.

ComponentAnnual QuotaWeightCommission basis
New Logo$1,500,00060%6% of revenue, accelerated (see §4)
Renewal$1,000,00040%3% of revenue, flat

4. Commission Structure

Commissions are paid as a percentage of booked revenue and calculated on a marginal (tiered) basis: revenue in each attainment band earns that band’s rate.

New Logo accelerators, by attainment of the New Logo quota:

Attainment of New Logo quotaCommission rate
0% – 100%6%
100% – 150%9%
150% and above12%

Renewal pays a flat 3% of revenue at all attainment levels (no accelerators).

5. Attainment Floor

No commissions are earned until the AE reaches 40% of total quota attainment (threshold floor). At and above 40%, commissions are paid per §4.

6. Payout Frequency

Commissions are calculated and paid quarterly.

7. Clawback

Standard clawback applies to cancellations within 6 months of booking.

8. Worked Example (100% attainment)

At exactly 100% of each component quota:

  • New Logo: $1,500,000 × 6% = $90,000
  • Renewal: $1,000,000 × 3% = $30,000
  • Total commission at 100% = $120,000, equal to the target variable.
